The DepEd Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

The DepEd Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where: Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.

STEM Strand

The STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) strand in our Academic Track is tailored for students passionate about science and technology. This program prepares them for future careers in fields such as engineering, computer science, biology, and physics.

Back

HUMSS Strand

The HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) strand in our Academic Track is tailored for students who love literature, social studies, and the arts. It is designed for those who intend to take up journalism, communication arts, liberal arts, education, and other social science-related courses in college.

Back

ABM Strand

Accountancy, Business, and Management is designed to prepare students for business-related college courses. It focuses on developing analytical skills, leadership, and practical business knowledge for future managers, accountants, or business owners.

Back

TVL Strand

The TVL Track is designed for students with a practical and hands-on approach to learning. Students can specialize in ICT, Home Economics, and SMAW, preparing them for immediate employment or further technical studies.
